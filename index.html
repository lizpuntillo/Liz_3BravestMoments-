My Brave Stories

Trying Out for the Team

Going into high school, everyone expected me to be a great basketball player like my sister. I hadn't played in two years but tried out because the coaches were pressuring me. I made the team that year, but ended up just being an average player. It turns out my sister got all the basketball genes and I haven't played since.

First Day of AP Euro

It was the first day of sophomore year and I was taking AP European History. The teacher was an old man that had been teaching that class for years and was known to be mean. I was so nervous the first day of his class that I didn't talk at all. To my surprise, he kept tallies on participation. After getting a zero on the first day of participation, I learned my lesson and began to speak up in his class. By the end of the semester I was a key contributor to our class discussions.

Running for Class Office

It was freshman year and I didn't plan on running for office until my mom found out about elections. She strongly encouraged me to at least try running for office, even if I knew I wouldn't get a position. I had to give a speech in front of my entire class of 150 people, most of whom I didn't know. In the end, I did not win a position but I did gain confidence in myself.
